About Volcanic Classes

Volcanic Classes, based in Patna, is a leading institute for IIT-JEE and NEET preparation — blending expert faculty, tech-powered learning, and personalized support to turn aspirations into achievements.

Founded by Mr. Anand Kumar (15+ years experience), Volcanic Classes has helped hundreds of students crack competitive exams with top ranks. We combine offline discipline with online flexibility to ensure uninterrupted learning. Our goal is not just exam success but to nurture thinkers, problem-solvers, and confident individuals.

With dedicated doubt support, exam-focused strategy, and value-driven mentorship, we prepare students not just for entrance tests — but for life beyond classrooms.

  • Experienced faculty with a passion for Physics education
  • Online + Offline hybrid learning with flexible options
  • Personalized mentorship and regular performance tracking
  • Real-time doubt solving, quizzes, and revision sessions
  • Recognized presence across Justdial, YouTube & Google
