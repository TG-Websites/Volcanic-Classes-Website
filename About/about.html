About Volcanic Classes

Where passion meets excellence, and dreams transform into achievements. Discover the story behind India's most trusted educational institution.

Our Story

Volcanic Classes began with a vision to make Physics engaging and accessible for every student. Founded in Patna by veteran educator Mr. Anand Kumar, our goal has always been to turn fear of Physics into fascination.

With 15+ years of teaching IIT-JEE and NEET aspirants, we focus on strong fundamentals, real-life applications, and personalized mentorship. Whether you're a board student or a dropper, our concept-driven approach ensures clarity, confidence, and consistent growth.

At Volcanic, we blend hybrid learning, expert faculty, and exam-oriented strategy to help students ignite their true academic potential.

Mission & Vision

Our Mission

My mission is to provide high-quality Physics education tailored to the unique demands of IIT-JEE and NEET aspirants. I am committed to nurturing analytical thinking, fostering curiosity, and building a strong foundation in fundamental concepts. By creating a supportive and engaging learning environment, I aim to help students maximize their potential and achieve their academic and career goals.

Our Vision

To ignite a deep understanding and passion for Physics in every student, empowering them with the conceptual clarity, problem-solving skills, and confidence needed to excel in competitive exams like IIT-JEE and NEET. I strive to make Physics relatable, enjoyable, and inspiring—transforming it from a subject of fear into one of fascination. Through a blend of innovative teaching methods, real-world applications, and consistent mentorship, I aim to build not just toppers, but thinkers, innovators, and lifelong learners.
