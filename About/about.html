Where passion meets excellence, and dreams transform into achievements. Discover the story behind India's most trusted educational institution.

Our Story

Founded in 2010 with a vision to revolutionize education in India, Volcanic Classes began as a small coaching center with just 20 students. Our founder, Dr. Rajesh Sharma, believed that every student deserves quality education regardless of their background.

What started as a humble initiative has now grown into one of India's most respected educational institutions, having guided over 10,000 students to success in competitive exams like IIT JEE, NEET, and Board examinations.

Our Journey

Milestones in our path to excellence

2010 - Foundation

Started with a vision to provide quality education to 20 students in a small classroom.

2013 - First IIT Success

Achieved our first IIT JEE Advanced selection, marking the beginning of our success story.

2016 - NEET Division Launch

Expanded our expertise to medical entrance preparation with dedicated NEET programs.

2019 - Digital Learning Platform

Launched our online learning platform, making quality education accessible nationwide.

2022 - Multiple Centers

Expanded to 5 centers across different cities, serving over 2000 students annually.

2025 - Excellence Recognition

Recognized as one of India's top coaching institutes with over 10,000 successful students.
